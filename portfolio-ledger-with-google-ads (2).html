Why the split works: Website clicks are priced far below calls or form fills because a click is the lowest-intent, lowest-cost action available — it fills the top of the funnel cheaply. Calls and form leads cost more per action because they're higher-intent, sales-ready conversions, so more of the per-action budget goes toward finding searchers who are ready to enquire, not just browse.
